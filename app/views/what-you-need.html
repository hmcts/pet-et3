What you need

Before you continue, make sure you have:

  • details of early conciliation, including the Acas certificate number
  • your case number - it's on the documents we sent you
  • your representative's details, if you have one

You'll also need the claimant's:

  • employment start and end dates
  • working hours and notice period
  • pay and benefits, before and after tax

You can upload 1 document with your full response. Make sure you have this ready and saved as a rich text file (.rtf).

If you don't enter any information for more than 60 minutes, you'll need to start again.
